Allison Green

David Zemach-Bersin leads the teaching staff of the New York City Feldenkrais Method Professional Training Program. He is the Program Director and primary teacher, and will be in attendance for 70-80% of the program. David is one of Dr. Moshé Feldenkrais's original students and studied closely with him from 1973-1984 in the U.S., Europe and at the Feldenkrais Institute in Israel. David is one of only 80 people to have completed his training entirely under Dr. Feldenkrais. He is one of the world's foremost Feldenkrais Teachers and Trainers, widely recognized for his clarity and generosity, and is the long time Director of the New York and Washington, D.C./Baltimore Feldenkrais Professional Training Programs. David is the Co-Founder of both The Feldenkrais Institute of New York and Feldenkrais Resources, the primary publisher of Dr. Feldenkrais’ English language materials. For over 25 years, he has maintained an active private practice, working with a diverse population, including those with severe limitations, neurological problems, infants, world-class musicians and those in chronic pain. David is an Honors graduate of the University of California Berkeley with extensive post-graduate work in physiological psychology, and is the Co-Author of Relaxercise (HarperCollins), a popular introduction to the Feldenkrais Method, and numerous Feldenkrais audio-tape programs. He has served as President of The Feldenkrais Guild, directed 15 Feldenkrais Professional Training Programs, and has taught in programs around the world. In addition, David has taught courses for physical and occupational therapists throughout the United States, and for has been involved in the development of innovative clinical and workplace applications of the Feldenkrais Method. David lives with his wife, writer and illustrator Kaethe, and their children in Bucks County, Pennsylvania.
